A network-science study of reputation, trust, and privacy in the sharing economy, conducted with the [Financial Computing &amp; Analytics Group](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/computer-science/research/research-groups/financial-computing-and-analytics) at University College London.

The project applied sentiment analysis and regression analysis to multivariate social media data to investigate relationships between user sentiment and platform dynamics in the sharing economy.
